Wireframing Project

Project Summary

Task

The purpose of this project was to conceptualize designs for the layout of our online portfolios. The goal of this project was to determine structure, layout, content, and functionality. We were required to hand-draw 8 wireframes and create 4 digital wireframes. Four of the hand-drawn wireframes and 2 of the digital wireframes were to be of the homepage, and the others were to be of a project page. The wireframes were to be completed individually.

Deliverable

The final product was 8 hand-drawn wireframes and 4 digital wireframes. Four of the hand-drawn wireframes and 2 of the digital wireframes were to be of the homepage, and the others were to be of a project page.

Timeframe

I had 1 week to create the 8 hand-drawn wireframes and 4 digital wireframes.

Constraints

The constraints of this project were that 8 hand-drawn wireframes were to be created first, and then 4 digital wireframes were to be created based off of what we liked about the hand-drawn wireframes.

Challenges

My main challenge with the wireframes was having to think about the most logical way to display information without any styling. In the past, whenever I have created a design, I first thought about how I wanted it to look and thought about layout after the fact. These wireframes forced me to think critically about how the user will gather information from the site. This way of thinking was very different for me, which presented a lot of challenges, but proved to be useful.
